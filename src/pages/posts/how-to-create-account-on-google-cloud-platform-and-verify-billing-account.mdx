### Step

1. Create Account

3. Resolve Suspended and Verify Account

## Create Account

Firstly visit the Google Cloud Website on Your Browser. After Create an Account
on GCP.

- [https://cloud.google.com/](https://cloud.google.com/)

![official Website](/images/post/2020/11/Cloud-Signup.png "official Website")

## Free Trial

You Create an Account successfully. Then Google Redirect To GCP Free Trial Page.

![Add Information](/images/post/2020/11/google-cloud-free-trial.png "Add Information")

1. Enter County
2. Allow Terms of Service
3. Continue
4. Account Type Business or Individual
5. Tax Information
6. Name And Address
7. Name
8. Address line 1
9. Address line 2
10. City
11. Postal Code
12. State
13. Primary Contact
14. Payment Method
15. Start My Trial

## Resolve Suspended and Verify Account

In some cases, We Try Multiple Time verify debit cards on GCP Or another issue.
Our New Account Temporarily Suspended and Google Cloud Team Send You A email To
say That Verify Account.

![Email Look Like](/images/post/2020/11/Verify-GCP-Account-1.png "Email Look Like")

TransitionAfter Open That Link GCP team Asks You Fill the form. Firstly you fill
Billing Account Id. Secondly, you Share Your Credit Card or debit card Bank
Statement with the last Four Digit Must be Show In Your Statement.Maybe you send
your credit card photos with the last 4 digits.

![Show Last 4 digit @copyright sbi.com](/images/post/2020/11/Create-Own-Blog-Thumb.png "Show Last 4 digit @copyright sbi.com")

<Alert>
SBI Card Credit Card E-Statement Not Show Your Last Four Digit. In case
You send Your E-Transaction History details with and in another case, you Go to
Bank Ask Bank staff to give my bank statements. 
</Alert>
