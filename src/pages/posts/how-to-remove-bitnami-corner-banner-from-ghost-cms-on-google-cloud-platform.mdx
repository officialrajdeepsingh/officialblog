You Install Your Ghost Cms Blog Help Of Bitnami.By Default Bitnami, Add
Corner Banner Logo On Your Website.

## **There are 3 steps:**

1. Access your Ghost instance
2. Enter the command to disable banner
3. Restart NGINX

### Access your Ghost instance

- Open Your Google Console Dashboard.

![VM INSTANCES](/images/post/2020/11/GCP-Dmain-2.png "VM INSTANCES")

- Open Your Google Console Dashboard Then Click Top Left Conner humber icon. Click Compute Engine, Select VM Instance, Then Your VM Instance Page Open.

---

![Instances](/images/post/2020/11/Ghost-SSH--1.png "Instances")

- After the Open Instance page, click SSH Button.

---

![Terminal](/images/post/2020/11/open-new-SSH-1.png "Terminal")

- After Open Your Instance Page. Then Click SSH Button. Some You See Open New window In button Side you See Your SSH terminal Successfully Open.

---

### Enter the command to disable banner && Restart NGINX

**First** **Command**

```bash
sudo /opt/bitnami/apps/ghost/bnconfig --disable_banner 1
```

---

**Second Command**

```bash
sudo /opt/bitnami/ctlscript.sh restart nginx
```

![Paste In terminal](/images/post/2020/11/rEMOVE-bANNER-lOGO.png "Paste In terminal")
